Role of ciliopathy genes in cilia

MSc student at Kaplan Lab since October 2023, investigating rare genetic diseases using C. elegans as a model organism. Her thesis research focuses on the DNAJC16 gene, and she is additionally investigating the wdr-31 gene to understand how its structural domains control dynamics at the ciliary base and preserve ciliary compartmentalization. A broader aim of her work is to understand the intricate relationship between cilia and mitochondria.
