Principal Investigators

Associate Professor with 15+ years in molecular genetics, cilia biology, and rare disease. He leads the Rare Disease Laboratory, combining C. elegans genetics, CRISPR/Cas9, advanced imaging, and open bioinformatics to study ciliopathies and neurodevelopmental disorders — working closely with clinical geneticists for translational impact.
