CiliaAtlas: Cell Type Ciliation Database

CiliaAtlas is a curated atlas classifying 359 human cell types across 13 organ systems and 83 organs by ciliation status. Each entry documents whether a cell type bears motile, primary, nodal, modified, or no cilia, with axonemal structure, cilia function, confidence level, and PubMed-linked evidence. Part of the Multidimensional Ciliary Atlas (MCA) project.

How to Use CiliaAtlas
  • Search: Enter any cell type name, organ, or organ system — results update instantly.
  • Filter: Use the dropdowns to narrow by organ system, ciliation status, cilia type, confidence level, or cilia function.
  • Sort: Click any column header to sort ascending; click again to reverse.
  • Cite: Click the 📋 button on any row to copy a formatted citation for that entry to your clipboard.
  • Reset: Click Reset All to clear all filters and reload the full atlas.
  • PubMed links: Each PMID in the last column is a direct link to the PubMed record.
  • Download: Export the full atlas or your current filtered view as a CSV file.
  • Ciliation statuses: Primary = single non-motile 9+0; Motile = multiple 9+2; Nodal = transient motile; Context-Dependent = varies by developmental stage or culture condition.
